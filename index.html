About UPTKOMP

UPTKOMP is an abbreviation of Unit Pelaksana Teknis Komputer. UPT Komputer is one of the work units at STMIK Widya Pratama Pekalongan. This unit is responsible for providing information technology infrastructure, both in terms of hardware and software, as well as internet availability. This unit is a support system for all departments / faculties in the university.
